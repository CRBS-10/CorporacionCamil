Our Process

1

Initial Consultation

We meet with you to understand your vision, requirements, and budget. Our team conducts a thorough site assessment and discusses design possibilities.

2

Design & Planning

Our architects and designers create detailed plans and 3D renderings. We handle all permits and ensure compliance with local building codes.

3

Construction Phase

Our skilled craftsmen begin construction with regular progress updates. We maintain clean, safe work sites and stick to agreed timelines.

4

Final Inspection & Handover

We conduct thorough quality checks and walk through the completed project with you. All warranties and maintenance information are provided.

Typical Timeline

Planning Phase

Foundation & Framing

Finishing Work

Total Project Duration: 3-6 months for typical residential projects
